# 🚀 ExpertHive

### Learn from Professionals. Grow with Real Experience.

ExpertHive is a mentorship and knowledge-sharing platform designed to connect **students, interns, and early-career professionals** with experienced **industry experts** for practical learning and career growth.

The platform helps learners gain **real-world insights beyond classrooms**, while enabling experts to **share knowledge, build personal brand, and monetize their experience.**

🌐 Live Website: https://experthive.co.in/

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

Many students and young professionals struggle to understand how industry actually works despite having academic knowledge.

At the same time, experienced professionals possess valuable practical insights but often lack a structured platform to share their expertise.

ExpertHive bridges this gap.

---

## 💡 Solution

ExpertHive provides a platform where users can:

🎓 Connect with industry professionals  
📅 Book mentorship or guidance sessions  
💬 Learn practical skills and career insights  
📈 Prepare for internships, interviews, and real projects  
💰 Enable experts to earn by sharing knowledge  

---

## ✨ Features

### For Students & Learners
- Search and discover industry experts
- Book 1:1 mentorship sessions
- Get practical industry exposure
- Receive career guidance
- Learn directly from experienced professionals

### For Experts
- Create expert profiles
- Manage availability
- Conduct mentoring sessions
- Earn through knowledge sharing
- Build professional credibility
